About


Hi, we are Appdictive and we create useful apps that you and your users will get addicted to ;)

Looking for app development?

We develop high quality native Android apps in cooperation with you. Together with you, and with the end users in mind, we design and implement your app idea - combining intuitive interfaces with fluid functionality.

We each have more than four years of experience in developing native Android apps and always seek to achieve the very best user experience to make your app valuable to the user. As a result of our efforts, our published apps have more than a million downloads in total to show for it!

We can also help you create Android versions of existing iPhone/iPad apps while staying true to the Android experience users expect and enjoy.
