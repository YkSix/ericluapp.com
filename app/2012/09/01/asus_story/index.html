ASUS Story Android app

Play Store

ASUS Story lets you collect and group your memorable photos into one insightful story. I worked for the main collage editor in this app.

Some showcases of the main collage editor:

Features of the collage editor:

  • Put multiple photos into one grid frame.
  • This app was developed with performance as the primary goal.
  • Change the grid size by simply dragging the border of the grid.
  • Simple touch gestures to zoom, resize, drag to delete, with fancy block animation.
  • Survived more than 24hr of focus monkey testing, with no crash and memory leaks detected.
